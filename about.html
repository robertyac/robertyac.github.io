Who I am

Hi, I'm Robert Yacovelli! I'm excited to share a little bit about myself with you.

Currently, I am a computer science student with a minor in data science at the University of British Columbia in Kelowna, BC. Here are some fun facts about me:

I'm fluent in French, thanks to attending a French immersion high school where I graduated valedictorian of my class.

I'm passionate about swimming, teaching others how to swim and water safety. I love the water, as a swimmer of my local swim club for over seven years this inspired me to become a coach and lifeguard/deck leader for the past three years. Swimming has taught me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance, all of which have helped me excel in my academic studies.

Over the past two years I have made Dean's list (maintained a sessional average of at least 85%) and received several scholarships and awards. Some languages and technologies I am familiar with include: Java, Python, R, Javascript, PHP, C, MIPS and SQL . I have experience with web/app development, data analysis, and higher-level mathematics such as probability theory and applied linear algebra. I have worked on various projects, such as creating a weather station dashboard, developpign a social media platform, and creating an app with a team.

If you want to know more about me or collaborate on a project, feel free to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
